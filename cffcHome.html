About The Company

The Custom Flip Flop Company is dedicated to providing high quality custom logo flip flops and sandals for almost any project, large or small. Our skilled craftsmen carefully create each customized pair by hand, ensuring your complete satisfaction and comfort. With over 20 years experience in the footwear market, we know you will be very happy with your own customized design. Depending on your specific requirements, we will produce your order in our facility in California, or at our partner factories in China and Mexico, on time and just the way you want (and we'll help you with the design, too!). Let us know if you have questions. We'll make it easy for you to understand all the details and how best to proceed. Thanks for checking out our website.
